How To Acknowledge Use Of TheHaloMod

Thank you very much for using TheHaloMod (or the hmf or halomod backends) for your research. We hope it has met your needs. If you have found it to be a useful tool, please let us know! The best way to do this is to cite Murray et al. (2020). If you used TheHaloMod primarily for the halo mass functions (or you got here by searching for HMFcalc), perhaps citing Murray et al. (2013) is more appropriate. {% endblock %}
